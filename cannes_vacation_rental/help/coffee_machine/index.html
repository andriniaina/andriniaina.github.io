Coffee machine

Espresso

  1. Fill water tank
  2. Switch the machine ON
  3. Wait for pressure build-up (green light 游릴).
  4. Unscrew the coffee receptacle.
  5. Put 1 tbsp of coffee
  6. Stamp the coffee
  7. Screw the coffee receptacle back
  8. Turn the main button to the right position “espresso”

Capuccino

  1. Fill water tank
  2. Switch the machine ON
  3. Turn the main button to the left position “capuccino”
  4. Wait for pressure build-up (green light 游릴).
  5. Make sure the steam valve is in “capuccino” position (down)
  6. Pour 1/3 milk in cup. Submerge the steam valve in the milk.
  7. Turn steam lever ON . Wait. Turn OFF.
  8. Add coffee by following the instructions for espresso (above).
